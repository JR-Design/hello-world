Problem


In Typography I was faced with the school project of creating a minimum 16 page booklet about any topic of our own choosing.

Project Brief


So of course I took the opportunity to write a booklet about my obsession of becoming a web designer/developer. I wrote this booklet for fellow students, teachers and anyone interested in getting into the web field. It was a great reflection of my current knowledge and general advice I wish I had when I first started.


I also got way into the idea of writing for the topic and forgot it was a typography project, So I used Helvetica.
