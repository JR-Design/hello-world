Problem


In this school project I had to conceptualize an idea to serve under represented people and create a promotional website and identity to display my solution to this issue. I chose to create a solution for students and disadvantaged youth.

Project Brief


With technology rapidly changing the environment that kids are growing, learning and being raised in I decided to create a solution for them to stay ahead of technology. I created circuits which is coding classes for students of all ages as well as a technology fund for disadvantaged youth


I chose a gender neutral color palette to appeal to both boys and girls, since STEM fields generally have issues attracting females. I also wanted to give off a friendly, professional, approachable feeling in the work.
