Problem


The Sudbury Branch of The Canadian Hard of Hearing Association host an annual “Ultimate Dream Home” Giveaway contest and due to Covid needed to drastically improve their website this year more than ever. They needed to appeal to a wider audience across ontario versus just the Sudbury market.

Project Brief


Originally their in-house designer was to produce the website design for my team to develop. Unfortunately his design was complex and not easily made responsive. We ended up having to create a new design from his graphics and apply a more modern approach while staying in the requirements from the Ontario Gaming Commission.


After many revisions we gained approval of the design from the client’s close eye and developed the website. I also had to integrate google tag manager to appease the needs of the outsourced marketing team and manage the VPS.

Deliverables


      Responsive Website Design
      Responsive Website Development
      Analytics, VPS management
