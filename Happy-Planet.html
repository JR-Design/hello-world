Problem


In my Package Design course I had to take a preexisting package design and improve upon it. As well in my motion graphics course I had to take a preexisting project and make a motion graphics project with it.

Project Brief


So I decided to make the new happy planet packaging more clear, friendly and inviting while also better displaying the real fruit ingredients in their smoothies. I felt this approach was strong and decided to create 4 different flavours and a 3D Image Mockup. I later took those files and taught myself Blender to create a 3D Motion Graphic Video allowing me to push this project further.
