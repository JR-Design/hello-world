Problem


In my first summer of working in the field after first year I was tasked with designing and developing a website for able computers. While the project itself is not portfolio worthy, It is simply to show where I started. This is my first ever website before I embarked into web design and development.

Project Brief


I wanted to explain all of able computers services, it’s rich local history and offer easy contact for people looking to do business with able computers. I wanted to make it feel professional and approachable, I used the colours from the logo and took the photos on the site mostly myself. In my second summer of work I made a new design but never developed it.
