Problem


Adanac Family Medicine was a newly opened business in Sudbury that needed to attract new business to their newly established practice. They wanted to be able to edit the website themselves so code was out of the question. Eventually they changed from a walk in clinic to only a family medicine clinic.

Project Brief


Working closely with the business owner I created multiple designs for their website. After having the design approved I developed the site using wordpress, set up the SEO and google anayltics to track the success of the site. I eventually refreshed their logo to remove the walk in clinic.
