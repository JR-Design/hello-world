Problem


Adanac Family Medicine was a newly opened business in Sudbury that needed to attract new patients to their practice. They wanted to be able to edit the website themselves, so code was out of the question. After working with them for a year they changed from a walk in clinic to only a family medicine practice and needed a logo refresh and the stationary design redone.

Project Brief


Working closely with the business owner I created multiple design options for their website. After the approval I developed the single page website using wordpress, set up the SEO and google analytics to track the success of the site. I eventually refreshed their logo to remove the walk in clinic and recreated their stationery design pieces.
