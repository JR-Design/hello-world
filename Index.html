Timeline/C.V.

  • Where I started

    I started experimenting with Photography where I gained an appreciation for fine art, imagery and colour.


  • Summer after University

    I worked as a marketing and sales associate in collaboration with a small business owner where I learnt first hand sales, marketing and fundamental business skills.

  • First Year Graphic Design

    After a successful summer of learning business development, with my background in photography I decided to pursue my interest in Graphic Design at Cambrian College.

  • My First Summer Job

    After my first year of school I went and got a full-time summer position at a startup technology company where I learnt general I.T., Web design and Development which was a life-long passion and interest of mine. I wore many different hats.

  • Second Year

    Trying to work/learn as a developer while returning to school I ended up focusing less on my school work and much more on self-teaching myself HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP and MySQL. Tutoring peers with my main focus of web design and frontend development made me learn, teach and collaborate with peers.

  • My Second Summer of Work

    I returned to my full-time summer position as a developer/designer where I felt that my knowledge was much more well rounded and I worked on more demanding complex projects.

  • Third Year

    I kept my self-teaching efforts up throughout the first semester starting to learn backend development such as Node.JS, Computing Fundamentals and Databases. In the second semester of the year I had a stronger focus on my graphic design work. All while tutoring my peers in web design/development.

  • Working while finishing School

    Returning to work this year I started to manage a small team, tackling larger projects while gaining the experience of managing someone and working within a team. After summer ended I returned to school part time to finish a couple courses while working full-time.
