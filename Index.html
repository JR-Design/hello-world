Timeline/C.V.

I started experimenting with Photography where I gained an appreication for fine art, imagery and colour.

I worked as a marketing and sales associate in collaboration with a small business owner where I learned first hand sales, marketing and fundamental buisness skills.

After a successful summer of learning business development, with my background in photography I decided to pursue my interest in graphic design at Cambrian College.

After my first year of school I went and got a full-time summer position at a startup technology company where I learnt general I.T, web design and development which was a life-long passion and interest of mine. I wore many different hats while working.

Trying to countine work as a developer while returning to school I ended up focusing less on my school work and much more on self-teaching myself HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP and MySQL. Tutoring peers with my strong knowledge on web design and frontend development allowed me to learn, teach and collobrate with peers.

I returned to my full-time summer position as a developer/designer where I felt that my knowledge was much more well rounded and I worked on more demanding complex projects.

Returning to school I kept my self-teaching efforts throughout the first semester learning backend development such as Node.JS, Computing Fundementals and Databases, while having a stronger focus on my graphic design work in the second semester of the year. All while continuing to tutor my peers in web design and development.
